FAQ

Can I get an estimate?

You sure can. Estimates are free for both lawn and window services. Contact us and make an appoint for your free estimate today!

How much will it cost to clean all the windows in my house?

The cost of the services varies greatly on factors like 1-2 stories, window size, glass doors, etc. As a general rule however, the going price is 5 dollars per window. This means both sides of a pane would cost 10 dollars. Multiply this amount by however panes of glass you have in your household, and you will get a general ballpark.

How much will it cost to mow my lawn?

We have a flat rate of $30 for every service, adding an additional $20 dollars for each hour after the first spent on mowing the lawn.

What areas do you provide your services too?

Our business Is based in Galt, Ca. Our services extend in an 18-mile radius in all directions from this point. This means our services reach Galt, Elk Grove, Rancho Cordova, and everything in between.

Are there any discounts?

We do, and you can make an account with us at request. This will ensure that your lawn is always kept up with, and you can opt-out of any service you feel that you don’t need. Most accounts are weekly or bi-monthly for lawn care, and every 3 months for window cleaning services.

Do I need to have the equipment?

Heavens no! Our service brings all our tools with us, so you don’t need to worry about anything.
